About the Creators

We built the UNT Campus Map Directory with the UNT community in mind; This could be for students, staff, and anybody else who calls UNT home. Being students overselves, we have many first-hand experiences that could have been solved, avoided, or simplified if we had access to an all-in-one UNT map.

This idea became the foundation for our project. Our goal is to make it easy for members of the UNT community to view and locate buildings, rooms, food options, recreational areas, and more.
